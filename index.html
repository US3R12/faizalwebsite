About Me

Hey, I’m Faizal! I’m an aspiring cybersecurity professional passionate about threat hunting, incident response, and digital forensics. My goal is to specialize in detecting, analyzing, and mitigating cyber threats to help organizations stay secure.

I’m currently working on my Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification and completing a cybersecurity program with Simplilearn to build a solid foundation in network security, penetration testing, and malware analysis. I spend a lot of time doing hands-on labs, CTF challenges, and real-world security exercises to sharpen my skills.

Beyond structured learning, I’ve been actively gaining experience through freelancing on Fiverr, where I offer cybersecurity services and help clients improve their digital security. I also explore Blue Team labs to strengthen my knowledge in security monitoring, log analysis, and incident response.

My journey into cybersecurity started out of pure curiosity—I’ve always been fascinated by how systems work and how attackers exploit them. Over time, this curiosity turned into a deep interest in defensive security, and now I’m working towards making it my career. I’m looking for entry-level cybersecurity roles in Bangalore (remote or onsite) where I can apply my skills, learn from experienced professionals, and grow in the field.

When I’m not diving into security, I enjoy learning about new attack techniques, improving my problem-solving skills, and staying up to date with cybersecurity trends. I believe cybersecurity is a field where learning never stops, and I’m always eager to explore new challenges.

Let’s connect and talk about all things security!
